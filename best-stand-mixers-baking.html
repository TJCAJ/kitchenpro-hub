Stand Mixer Buying Guide

Key Features to Consider

  • Bowl Capacity: 4.5-5 qt for home bakers, 6+ qt for large families or frequent baking
  • Motor Power: 300W minimum, 500W+ for heavy doughs and large batches
  • Mixing Action: Planetary mixing ensures thorough ingredient incorporation
  • Speed Settings: 10+ speeds provide precise control for different tasks
  • Attachments: Dough hook, wire whip, flat beater should be included

Popular Attachments

  • Food Grinder: Perfect for fresh sausages and grinding meat
  • Pasta Roller: Create fresh pasta at home
  • Ice Cream Maker: Homemade ice cream and sorbet
  • Spiralizer: Vegetable noodles and creative preparations

Stand Mixer Care and Maintenance

Daily Care

  • Wipe down exterior with damp cloth after each use
  • Wash bowl and attachments in warm, soapy water
  • Ensure mixer is completely dry before storing
  • Store attachments in a convenient location

Long-term Maintenance

  • Check for loose screws and tighten as needed
  • Lubricate moving parts annually (if recommended)
  • Replace worn attachments when performance declines
  • Professional service every 5-10 years for heavy users
